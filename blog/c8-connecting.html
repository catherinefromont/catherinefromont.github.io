Complete the political awareness exercise here in writing.

Well, this weekend my mum is taking myself and my two siblings away on holiday for a week. She feels as though the other two don't appreciate what shes done for them, shes spent time and money prepping this awesome holiday for us, and my brother has taking 4 days of it by inviting 4 friends around, and my sister wants to go out every night. Now I looked at this situation from my mums perspective, and from my perspective. I talked to my siblings about how mum would be feeling because of the way they are treating her, and I told them to try and put themselves in her position - and imagine how she must be feeling. I did that and it was a huge wake up call. For them it was too. That showed Political Awareness on my behalf and the conflict was resolved shortly after doing this exercise.
