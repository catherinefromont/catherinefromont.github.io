What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness is the way we are able to reflect on our own emotional standings, whereas mindfulness is having the ability of pay attention and take action on your emotions in order for you to be able to show self awareness and reflect upon them. They are very similar, but help each other out to become one.
