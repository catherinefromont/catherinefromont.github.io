C1 Reflection Blog

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

I think its awesome, different and I hope it takes off and more and more people get on board with the way EDA/DBC does things.

What are your impressions?

Great so far!

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I am coming into this with an open mind, not knowing what to expect but I will engage to the best of my ability.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

Yes! Its better than I expected!

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Yes I want to challenge myself and i think that developing the skills to learn in different ways will make me more adaptable if I am ever thrown into an unfamiliar environment. I am excited!!

C1 Time and Habits Blog

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I always try my best to be mindful and the Pomodoro technique. But I tend to spend half an hour focussing and then having a break and repeat that. Helps me focus so much.

What did you find interesting?

How many different techniques there are and how some work for some people but not for others

What worked for you?

Pomodoro technique.

Which will you experiment further with?

Maybe I will try some forms of meditation, to help me unwind when I am stressed.
