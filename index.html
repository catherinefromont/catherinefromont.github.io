About Me

My name is Catherine Fromont, I am 20 years old and currently studying here at Dev Academy! I am based in Cambridge in the Waikato at the moment working as a Calf Rearer on a farm full time, but when I do get time off I am studying. I have recently just completed a Diploma in Web Development and Design, so this isn't my first time coding. I really enjoy it!! Looking forward to meeting you all in January.
